ME

Hello, my name is Samuel Mandeng. I'm a web developer and more generally a computer enthusiast.

Realizing this site is a way for me to be an actor in the fight against covid 19. Considering the number of hundreds of thousands and now millions of people who are affected by this disease, I proposed to realize this website for two main objectives :

  • to remind everyone that covid19 is real and that it does not only affect others.
  • allowing everyone to easily access data about the evolution of covid19 around the world

RESOURCES (,)
